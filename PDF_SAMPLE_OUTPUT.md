## Key Design Features

### ✅ Professional Header
- Company logo placeholder (🏢)
- Bold company name
- Address and phone
- Clean horizontal divider

### ✅ Clear Period Display
- Highlighted bordered box
- Easy-to-read period range
- Supports custom date ranges

### ✅ Well-Organized Tables
- Dark header rows (dark blue-gray #2c3e50)
- Alternating row colors for easy reading
- Clean borders and proper spacing
- Right-aligned currency values

### ✅ Financial Accuracy
- All calculations shown explicitly
- Rounding & excess tracked
- Void/cancelled items highlighted in red
- Total revenue highlighted with darker background

### ✅ Payment Method Filtering
- Only shows actual payment modes
- Excludes "UNSPECIFIED" and "UNKNOWN" automatically
- Sorted alphabetically for easy auditing
- Real-time aggregation from database

### ✅ Professional Footer
- Signature lines for audit trail
- Print date and time
- "Powered by UNIPRO" branding
- Automatic page numbering

✅ **Multi-Page**: Automatic page breaks, header repetition  
✅ **Print Ready**: Optimized for laser and inkjet printers  

## Data Aggregation

All financial data is automatically calculated from the database:

- **Net Sales**: Sum of all item amounts (excluding voids)
- **Service Charge**: Sum of service charge field
- **Tax Collected**: Sum of GST/tax amounts
- **Rounding & Excess**: Sum of rounding adjustments
- **Total Revenue**: Net Sales + Service Charge + Tax + Rounding
- **Total Orders**: Count of settlements
- **Total Items**: Sum of item quantities
- **Total Discount**: Sum of discount amounts
- **Voids**: Count and amount of voided items
- **Payment Breakdown**: Grouped by normalized payment mode
